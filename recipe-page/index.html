BANANA FOSTER FONDUE

Banana foster fondue make me happier when I'm eating it. I tried it in Melting Pot and the taste likes it will watering on your mouth than other recipe. I think Banana Foster Fondue is the best choice of melting chocolate.

  • Prep Time: 35 min
  • Serving: 6 people

    • Ingredients

    • 2 TBL. Butter
    • 1/2 c. Light brown sugar
    • Sprinkle of Cinnamon
    • 1/8 tsp. Vanilla extract
    • 1/8 c. Banana Liqueur
    • 1/8 c. Captain Morgan's Spiced Rum
    • 7-8oz of Melted White Chocolate
    • 1/4 oz 151 rum

    This mixture can be refrigerated for up to 7 days.

    Please Click me!!!!

    Procedue

    1. melt butter over low heat
    2. add brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla
    3. allow mixture to simmer and bubble until sugar completely melts
    4. add as much or as little of this mixture to the melted white chocolate.
    5. top with bananas if desired
    6. use 151 to flambe. Sprinkle a little cinnamon while flambiing for a sparkle effect!
