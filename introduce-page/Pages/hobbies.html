OUTDOOR ACTIVITES

Skiing is my favorite activity in the winter. Skiing help me to challenges myself to become a stronger person, overcome the scare of height and enjoying the wonderful landscape when I'm at the top of the mountain.

Tennis is my best choice in summer. I learned from my friend and can play it after a month. Tennis is good excersice and team enviroment.

Rock Climbing is a sport I really wanted to try when I lived in Vietnam. Climbing is really fun and never boring. They often set up routes on the walls and change them every month. They have many routes with different level for you to challenges yourselft. There are so many skills to acquire to train your body and mind. It's perfect for explorer.
